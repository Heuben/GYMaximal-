Body Building

A sport and practice that involves the intensive use of resistance exercise to control and develop one's musculature. The primary goal of bodybuilding is to build muscle size and strength, create an aesthetically pleasing physique, and enhance overall physical fitness. This is achieved through a combination of weight training, proper nutrition, and adequate rest.

Bench Press

This exercise is mainly used to build muscle and increase strength. The bench press is a compound exercise that works your chest and shoulders, as well as the triceps..

Inclined Bench Press

The purpose of the incline press is to focus more of the work on the upper pecs. The main benefit in performing incline presses is to develop the upper portion of the pectoral muscles.

Declined Bench Press

It consists of the clavicular head (upper pec) and sternal head (lower pec). The purpose of the decline bench press is to work the lower pecs

Peck Deck Fly

A pec deck machine provides a fairly simple and effective workout for building your major chest muscles.

Shoulder Press

The biggest benefactor of the shoulder press is the front portion of your shoulder muscle (anterior deltoid) but you'll also be working out your deltoids, triceps, trapezius and pecs

Lateral Raises

Raising your arms to the side in a plane-like position is also called a shoulder abduction. Lateral raises muscles worked primarily include the lateral deltoid muscles.

Arm Cable Side Raises

Arm Cable Side Raise. This middle delt exercise is a fantastic unilateral variation of the standard side raise.

Bent-over Dumbell Flyes

Bent over dumbbell fly, is an isolated strength training exercise, used to target the posterior or rear deltoid.

Bicep Curl

Consistently performing the bicep curl will help you build strength in the upper arm and learn to use your arm muscles correctly, bracing with your core muscles.

Hammer Curl

Hammer curls target the long head of the bicep as well as the brachialis (another muscle in the upper arm) and the brachioradialis (one of the key forearm muscles).

Skull Crusher

Skull crushers work all three heads of the tricep and can be a helpful tool for building strength and size in the arms.

Tricep Overhead Extension

The overhead tricep extension is a great way to target all three heads on the triceps, as well as improve shoulder and core stability.

Bent Over Row

The bent over row primarily works the latissimus dorsi (the large wing-like muscles in your back), the middle and lower trapezius, the rhomboids, and the posterior deltoids.

Barbell Squat

A squat is a strength exercise in which the trainee lowers their hips from a standing position and then stands back up
